Who it's for

Built with workers, in many languages

👷‍♂️

Dormitory workers

Construction and manufacturing

  • Long shifts, short breaks
  • Budget around $3-$6 per meal
  • Mostly eat at worksite canteens
  • Halal or vegetarian options needed
  • Limited or expensive mobile data
How we help: Offline canteen picks and simple budget tracking
👩‍🍳

Domestic workers

Caregivers and household staff

  • Little control over menu
  • Must consider employer's tastes
  • Keep own dietary rules
  • Need affordable, healthy recipes
  • Very limited time to cook
How we help: 5-10 minute, family-friendly recipes with low cost per serve
🌙

Shift & night workers

Security, cleaning, logistics, delivery

  • Irregular hours
  • Eat at hawker centres/mini-marts
  • Need fast, healthy choices
  • No time to plan ahead
  • Must stay within budget
How we help: “Best choice” cards and instant suggestions for hawker stalls
